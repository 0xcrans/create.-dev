WHY?

CreativesDAO, the main sponsor, is sun-setting its Trust. To leverage its remaining treasury of 1995 NEAR for the benefit of the community, the Trustees have decided to launch the Create./Dev Movement with Potlock's assistance. This initiative focuses solely on creative-technical projects, aiming to support innovations that blend technology and art.

Potlock provides an innovative capital allocation mechanism known as Quadratic Funding. This system amplifies the impact of community donations by using a mathematical formula to match individual contributions with funds from a larger pool. It gives more weight to the number of contributors rather than the size of each donation, ensuring a more democratic distribution of funds.

The movement seeks individuals or groups with an artist's heart and a developer's mind, embodying the true essence of Create./Dev. To maintain quality and value, the following rules apply:

  2. Applicants can be individuals (artists/developers) or DAOs
